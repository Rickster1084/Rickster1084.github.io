Napoleon's Vietnam

Watch one of our fan favorites Napoleon's Vietnam. This video focuses on the Peninsular War, this war tied down over a million badly needed French troops in a guerilla Vietnam style of combat that would not end until the French were drove out of the Spanish countryside by the joint forces of the Spanish, Portuguese, and British Armies. This video gives you an in-depth look at the tactics used by the allied armies, Wellington's strategy, and ultimately one of Napoleon's worse decision's.
